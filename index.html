Travel Paws

Take your pet on an unforgettable trip

    THE PROBLEM

    How to find a trustworthy accommodation, activity, or dine-in option that’s pet-friendly? Pet-owner commit excessive time to search for locations that is welcoming to their pet especially when they are traveling. There should be an open platform for them to find their ideal pet-friendly location to stay and play at.

    PROPOSED SOLUTION

    Travel Paws will provide users with reliable pet-friendly travel accommodations, restaurants, and activities with hours, user reviews, and detailed map to streamline the research process for pet-owners who wants to have a flawless trip. They can easily search and book for a preferred location to be prepared for their upcoming trip at anytime, anywhere.
