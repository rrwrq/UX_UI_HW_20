Travel Paws

Take your pet on an unforgettable trip

Travel Paws APP is a platform where users will be able to find pet-friendly locations like accommodations, restaurants, and parks.

Project Overview
Description

Our pet-friendly travel app provide users with countless locations that are pet-friendly, which will affect pet-owners who want a flawless and relaxing trip by viewing all pet-friendly locations in one app.

We will measure effectiveness by analyzing how many users book a stay or went to each location using this app.

Problem Statement & Challenges
Description

Pet owners devote excessive time to search for pet-friendly travel activities, accommodations, and dine-in options that are suitable for their pets

“I find it troublesome to take my dog along the trip because I need to do time-consuming research. Even after investing considerable time into this preparation, I’m still left with numerous worries and concerns” — Jane (Interviewee)

Solution

Our app will provide users with reliable information, user reviews, and detailed map to streamline the research process for pet-friendly travel accommodations, restaurants, and activities

Interview Plan

The objective of this interview plan is to gather insights and feedback from potential users to understand their needs, pain-points, and goals for a pet-friendly traveling app. This information will help in creating the app to better serve the target audience. I will start with introducing myself and explaining the purpose of this interview. I will then ask the participant easy and welcoming questions first then move on to the more specific questions
